Spider Man

Real name
Peter Benjamin Parker
First appearance
August, 1962 in Amazing Fantasy #15
Created by:
Stan Lee & Steve Ditko
Origin
Peter Parker was an ordinary high school student from Queens, New York, who gained spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Initially using his powers for personal gain, Peter tragically learned the cost of irresponsibility when his Uncle Ben was killed. Inspired by the lesson that “with great power comes great responsibility,” he became Spider-Man, dedicating his life to fighting crime while balancing the struggles of a normal life.
Notable Storylines
1. The Death of Gwen Stacy, one of the most iconic and tragic arcs in comic history.
2. The Clone Saga, introducing characters like Ben Reilly and Kaine.
3. Spider-Verse, where Peter teams up with alternate versions of Spider-Man across the multiverse.
